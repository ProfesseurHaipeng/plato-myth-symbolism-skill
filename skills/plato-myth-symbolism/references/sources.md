## Source protocol

Record dialogue, Stephanus range, language, translation, host, access date, displayed licence, and whether a statement is a quotation or paraphrase. The repository stores source metadata and short analytic summaries; it does not bulk-copy translations.

| `perseus-republic-cave` | Cave source and stable example locator | [Republic VII, 514a](https://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=plat.+rep.+7.514a) | Link and quote sparingly with translation attribution. |

Do not fabricate Greek. A Greek term belongs in an answer only when the relevant card or a verified primary text supplies its locator and translation context.
