# Classical corpus and citation protocol

## Required citation fields

Every analytic claim must record dialogue, Stephanus start/end, speaker, audience, translation, source URL, and whether the wording is quotation or paraphrase.

## Core learning corpus

| Unit | Dialogue | Stephanus | What to learn |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cave | *Republic* VII | 514a–521b | education, knowledge, return to the city |
| Ring of Gyges | *Republic* II | 359a–360d | justice and invisibility thought experiment |
| Noble lie | *Republic* III | 414b–415d | political myth and civic formation |
| Myth of Er | *Republic* X | 614b–621d | choice, responsibility, afterlife narrative |
| Aristophanes' humans | *Symposium* | 189c–193d | comic speech, eros, wholeness |
| Diotima's ladder | *Symposium* | 201d–212c | eros and ascent to beauty |
| Charioteer | *Phaedrus* | 246a–257b | soul, desire, rhetoric, recollection |
| Theuth | *Phaedrus* | 274c–275b | writing, memory, dialectic |
| Phaedo geography | *Phaedo* | 107c–115a | soul, mythic geography, limits of proof |
| Gorgias judgment | *Gorgias* | 523a–527a | justice, rhetoric, post-mortem judgment |
| Kronos | *Statesman* | 268d–274e | cosmic reversal and politics |
| Atlantis | *Timaeus* / *Critias* | 20d–26e / continuation | political narrative and reception |

Use a primary-text host such as [Perseus](https://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/collection?collection=Perseus%3Acorpus%3Aperseus%2Cauthor%2CPlato) or [Scaife Viewer](https://scaife.perseus.org/). Link rather than bulk-copy translations.

## Minimum card fields

Every card in the starter corpus includes dialogue, Stephanus start/end, speaker, audience, narrative status, argument context, source id, analysis prompts, and non-claims. A model must locate the record before treating it as evidence.
